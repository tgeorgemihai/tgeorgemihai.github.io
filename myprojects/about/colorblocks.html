Puzzle Platformer (Color Blocks)

Controls:

press arrow_backarrow_forward keyboard keys to move left and right

press arrow_upward keyboard key to jump

touch star or press [X] keyboard key to store color, white cube or use stored color on white cube

touch close to discard the current stored color

touch zoom_out_map to enable/disable fullscreen

touch home to return to main menu

Collect all the coins to finish the level.

Get color from paint buckets and use it on white cubes to change their movement.

You can steal the color of a cube to make it white.

White cubes can be given color. But also can be stored and moved.
